## Authentication

### Register

1. On the site's home-page, click the big "Try Amusix" button

   <img alt="Home-page register" src="images/screenshots/homepage_register.png" style="width: 750px"/>

2. Fill the mandatory fields of the displayed form:
    * Username:
        * Unique username to identity yourself through the application
        * At least 5 characters long
        * Only lowercase characters
        * At least one lowercase alphabetic character
        * Can contain digits
        * Can contain "-" and "_"
    * Display name: name others will see in the application
    * Password:
        * Password to access the application
        * At least 6 characters long
        * At least one digit between 0 and 9
        * At least one uppercase character
        * At least one lowercase character
        * At least one special character

    <img alt="Register" src="images/screenshots/register.png" style="width: 400px; border: 1px solid gainsboro"/>

3. Confirm your password then click "Register"

4. If the registration succeed, you will be redirected to the log-in form

### Log in

1. On the site's home-page, click the "Log in" button at the top right of the page

    <img alt="Home-page login" src="images/screenshots/homepage_login.png" style="width: 750px"/>

2. Fill the displayed form by providing your username and password, then click "Log in"

    <img alt="Login" src="images/screenshots/login.png" style="width: 400px; border: 1px solid gainsboro"/>

3. If the authentication succeed, you will be redirected to your playlists plage

## Songs

### Search songs

1. In the search bar bellow the application's header, enter the search text corresponding to the song you're looking for, then click "Search"

    <img alt="Search bar" src="images/screenshots/search_bar.png" style="width: 400px; border: 1px solid gainsboro"/>

2. The results corresponding to your search text will be listed in a dedicated page

    <img alt="Search results" src="images/screenshots/search-results.png" style="width: 750px; border: 1px solid gainsboro"/>

> [!TIP]
> Scroll to the bottom of the list (or click "Load more") to load more results

### Play song

1. Hover or click on one of the songs from the result list and click on the play button

    <img alt="Play song" src="images/screenshots/play_song.png" style="width: 400px"/>

2. The song player will open to play the selected song

    <img alt="Player" src="images/screenshots/player.png" style="width: 400px"/>

### Add song to a playlist

1. Hover or click on one of the songs from the result list and click on the plus button

    <img alt="Add song" src="images/screenshots/add_song.png" style="width: 400px"/>

2. A window will appear to let you add the song into an existing or new playlist

#### Add song to an existing playlist

1. In the window, select the "Existing playlist" tab

2. Select the playlist you want to add the song in, then click "Add song to playlist"

    <img alt="Existing playlist modal" src="images/screenshots/existing_playlist_modal.png" style="width: 400px; border: 1px solid gainsboro"/>

3. The song will be added to the selected playlist

#### Add song to a new playlist

1. In the window, select the "New playlist" tab

2. Enter the name of the playlist to create with song in, then click "Add song to new playlist"

    <img alt="New playlist modal" src="images/screenshots/new_playlist_modal.png" style="width: 400px; border: 1px solid gainsboro"/>

3. The playlist will be created with the song added in

    <img alt="Update playlist" src="images/screenshots/update_playlist.png" style="width: 400px"/>

    <img alt="Rename playlist" src="images/screenshots/rename_playlist.png" style="width: 400px; border: 1px solid gainsboro"/>

## Song player

### Full screen player

The full screen song player will open when playing a song or when [clicking the "^" button in the minimized player](#display-full-screen-player)

The player displays the playing song's thumbnail, name and artist name, and also offers controls for song progression and volume level

<img alt="Full screen player" src="images/screenshots/full_screen_player.png" style="width: 750px"/>

#### Minimize player

Click the "v" button in the top right corner of the player to minimize it 

<img alt="Minimize player" src="images/screenshots/minimize_player.png" style="width: 450px"/>

### Minimized player

The minimized song player can be displayed by [clicking on the "^" button in the full screen player](#minimize-player) 

The player only displays the song progression bar and controls, which minimizes it enough to let you do other tasks while still listening to a song

<img alt="Minimized player" src="images/screenshots/minimized_player.png" style="width: 750px; border: 1px solid gainsboro"/>

#### Display full screen player

Click the "^" button at the right of the player to display it in full screen

<img alt="Display full screen player" src="images/screenshots/display_full_screen_player.png" style="width: 450px"/>

### Play / pause song

In the player click on the play / pause button to:

* Pause the playing song

    <img alt="Pause song" src="images/screenshots/pause_song.png" style="width: 450px"/>

* Resume the paused song

    <img alt="Resume song" src="images/screenshots/resume_song.png" style="width: 450px"/>

* Restart the song from the beginning if it ended

    <img alt="Restart song" src="images/screenshots/restart_song.png" style="width: 450px"/>

### Change song progression

The player's progression bar displays the playing song progression

Click anywhere on the bar to change the song progression to the corresponding time

<img alt="Change song progression" src="images/screenshots/change_song_progression.png" style="width: 450px"/>

> [!NOTE]
> When viewing the minimized player on a mobile device screen, the song progression will only be displayed as read-only

### Control volume

#### Change volume level

In the minimized player, hover the volume button and click anywhere on the displayed volume bar to change the volume level

<img alt="Minimized player volume control" src="images/screenshots/minimized_player_volume_control.png" style="width: 450px"/>

In the full screen, directly click anywhere on the displayed volume bar to change the volume level

<img alt="Full screen player volume control" src="images/screenshots/full_screen_player_volume_control.png" style="width: 450px"/>

#### Mute / unmute volume

In the player, click once on the volume button to mute the volume

<img alt="Mute volume" src="images/screenshots/mute_volume.png" style="width: 250px"/>

Click again to unmute the volume

<img alt="Unmute volume" src="images/screenshots/unmute_volume.png" style="width: 250px"/>

### Close player

Click on the cross in the right / top right corner of the player to close it and stop the playing song

<img alt="Close player" src="images/screenshots/close_player.png" style="width: 450px"/>

<img alt="Consult profile" src="images/screenshots/consult_profile.png" style="width: 400px"/>

The page displays some information about your profile

### Log out

1. In your profile page, click the "Log out" button

    <img alt="Log out" src="images/screenshots/logout.png" style="width: 400px; border: 1px solid gainsboro"/>

2. Confirm that you want to log out of the application

    <img alt="Confirm log out" src="images/screenshots/confirm_logout.png" style="width: 400px; border: 1px solid gainsboro"/>

3. You will be logged out and redirected to the application's home-page

    <img alt="Change password" src="images/screenshots/change_password.png" style="width: 400px; border: 1px solid gainsboro"/>

    <img alt="Password change modal" src="images/screenshots/password_change_modal.png" style="width: 400px; border: 1px solid gainsboro"/>

3. Confirm your password change

    <img alt="Confirm password change" src="images/screenshots/confirm_password_change.png" style="width: 400px; border: 1px solid gainsboro"/>

4. You will be logged out and prompted to log in again with your new password
